Navada WaterFalls
Mary Jane Falls is a beautiful waterfall located in the Spring Mountains near Las Vegas, Nevada. The hike to the falls is about 3 miles round trip and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The waterfall itself cascades down a rocky cliff, creating a refreshing spot to relax and enjoy nature.

Indonesia
